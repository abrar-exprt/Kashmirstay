Top Attractions in Pahalgam

Each valley, meadow and river in Pahalgam is a landscape painting brought to life.

Scenic Valley · Bollywood
Betab Valley
Named after the 1983 Bollywood blockbuster filmed here — a stunning open meadow with snow peaks, pine forests and a silver stream. 15-minute drive from Pahalgam town.
Meadow · Pony Access Only
Baisaran (Mini Switzerland)
Pahalgam's most magical spot — a vast green bowl at 2,440m accessible by pony (₹400–₹600) or foot. Surrounded by pine forests and Himalayan peaks — every visitor understands the 'mini-Switzerland' nickname instantly.
Trek Base · Wildlife
Aru Valley
A pristine alpine valley 12 km from Pahalgam — the starting base for Great Lakes Trek and Kolahoi Glacier expeditions. Meadows, Himalayan wildlife and traditional Gujjar shepherd villages.
River Rafting · Fishing
Lidder River
Turquoise Lidder River roaring through Pahalgam — perfect for white-water rafting (Grade I–III, ₹600–₹1,200/person), trout fishing and romantic riverside walks at dusk.
Snow Year-Round · Amarnath
Chandanwari
Starting point of the Amarnath Yatra and famous for permanent snow — sledge rides available even in July. The snow-play experience for families and kids, just 15 km from Pahalgam.
High Altitude · Sacred
Sheshnag Lake
Sacred alpine lake at 3,584m on the Amarnath Yatra trail — named for its serpent shape. One of Kashmir's most spectacular high-altitude destinations for serious trekkers.

Pahalgam FAQ

How far is Pahalgam from Srinagar? +
Pahalgam is 96 km from Srinagar — approximately 2.5–3 hours by road through the scenic Lidder Valley. Private taxis cost ₹2,500–₹3,500 one-way. Shared Sumos from TRC bus stand ₹400–₹600/person. Most tour agencies include transfers in Pahalgam packages.
What is Baisaran (mini-Switzerland) in Pahalgam? +
Baisaran is a vast alpine meadow 3 km from Pahalgam town at 2,440m — accessible only by pony (₹400–₹600 round trip) or on foot. Surrounded by pine forests and Himalayan peaks on all sides, it earned the 'mini-Switzerland' nickname from the thousands of visitors who gasp at first sight.
Is Pahalgam good for a honeymoon? +
Absolutely. Pahalgam is Kashmir's most romantic destination — Lidder River sunset walks, Baisaran meadow picnics, Betab Valley photo sessions, and cosy mountain resort stays. Best honeymoon season: April–June (spring flowers) and September–October (golden autumn).
What is the best trek from Pahalgam? +
The Great Lakes Trek (7 alpine lakes, 7–9 days) is the most famous — stunning, moderate-to-challenging. Shorter options: Baisaran meadow (3 km, easy), Aru Valley (5 km loop, easy), Tulian Lake (14 km return, moderate). All accessible through MBP-verified agencies.
