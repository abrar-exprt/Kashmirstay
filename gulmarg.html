Activities in Gulmarg

From powder skiing in winter to wildflower trekking in summer — Gulmarg delivers adventure in every season.

❄️ Nov – Mar
Skiing & Snowboarding
World-class powder skiing on Himalayan slopes. 5 km main runs, off-piste options on Apharwat. Beginner lessons from ₹1,500/hr, ski rentals available on-site.
🌸 May – Oct
Horse Riding & Trekking
Ride through emerald meadows carpeted with wildflowers toward Khilanmarg and Alpather Lake. Trek to Ningle Nallah waterfall. Most beautiful in June–August.
❄️ Dec – Feb
Snowmobile & Sledge Rides
Zoom across Gulmarg's snow plains on a snowmobile (₹500–₹800/ride) or take a classic horse-drawn sledge through the winter wonderland. Kids love it!
⛳ Jun – Sep
World's Highest Golf Course
Gulmarg Golf Course (2,650m) is one of the world's highest — 18 holes surrounded by Himalayan peaks and pine forests. A bucket-list experience for golf lovers.
🌿 Apr – Sep
Gulmarg Biosphere Reserve
A 180 sq km UNESCO Biosphere Reserve teeming with Himalayan wildlife — snow leopards, hangul deer, golden eagles. Guided nature walks and birdwatching tours available.
🏔️ Jun – Aug
Alpather Lake Trek
A stunning high-altitude frozen lake beyond the Gondola Phase 2 — the 4 km trek from Apharwat station reveals sweeping glacial views and remains frozen even in July.

Your Questions

Gulmarg FAQ

What is the Gulmarg Gondola ticket price in 2025? +
Phase 1 (Gulmarg→Kongdori): ₹600–₹750/person. Phase 2 (Kongdori→Apharwat 3,980m): ₹800–₹1,000/person. Combined Phase 1+2: ₹1,200–₹1,500/person. Prices are seasonal and subject to JKTDC revision. Book online or through a verified agency to avoid queues.
When is the best time to ski in Gulmarg? +
Best skiing: December–February with heavy snowfall (2–5 feet). November and March have good snow with shorter queues. April offers spring skiing at Apharwat peak. Summer (May–October) is for trekking, golf and horse riding in the Biosphere Reserve meadows.
Can beginners ski in Gulmarg? +
Absolutely. Gulmarg has dedicated beginner slopes at Phase 1 (Kongdori). Ski school instructors offer lessons from ₹1,500/hour, and full equipment rental (skis, boots, poles, helmets) is available. Most agencies include lessons in ski packages for first-timers.
How far is Gulmarg from Srinagar and how do I get there? +
Gulmarg is 56 km from Srinagar — 1.5–2 hours by road. Options: private taxi ₹2,000–₹2,500 one-way; shared Sumo from Tangmarg for ₹150–₹300; full-day agency tour ₹3,500–₹5,000. No direct bus; most tourists go by cab or with a tour agency.
