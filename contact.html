About Us

Kashmir's Most Trusted Travel Platform

KashmirStay is built on the MBP (Make Business Prominent) platform — a Srinagar-based travel directory connecting tourists from 15+ countries to verified, government-registered Kashmir agencies since 2022.

We are not a booking OTA. We are a verification and discovery platform — we rigorously check each listed agency for J&K Tourism registration, proper licensing, real tourist reviews and safety compliance before listing them.

Our AI planner at makemetop.in/help-ai.html is free to all tourists — it uses local Kashmir expertise to build personalised itineraries, budget guides and agency recommendations in under 60 seconds.
