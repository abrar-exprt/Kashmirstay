Srinagar · Houseboats · Shikara
Dal Lake, Srinagar
Float through history on a heritage wooden houseboat. Watch the sunrise over the Himalayas from your bedroom window, take a shikara through floating gardens, and shop at the famous Dal Lake floating market.

Ski Capital
Skiing · Gondola · Snow Sports
Gulmarg
Asia's highest gondola cable car at 3,980m. World-class skiing, snowboarding and snow activities from November to March. Stunning summer trekking and horse riding April to October.
Gondola from ₹600/person
→

Valley of Shepherds
Valleys · Trekking · Honeymoon
Pahalgam
Betab Valley, Baisaran mini-Switzerland meadow, Aru Valley and the crystal-clear Lidder River. The base for Amarnath Yatra and one of Kashmir's most romantic destinations.
Day trips from ₹1,800/person
→

Meadow of Gold
Glaciers · Camping · Ladakh Gateway
Sonamarg
The Meadow of Gold at 2,740m. Thajiwas Glacier snow activities, Zero Point adventure, and the gateway to Leh Ladakh road trip via the legendary Zoji La pass.
Tours from ₹2,200/person
→
🏔️
Land of High Passes
Leh · Pangong Lake · Nubra Valley
Leh Ladakh
The high-altitude Himalayan desert that stops your breath — in every sense. Pangong Lake's impossible blue, Nubra Valley's sand dunes, ancient Buddhist monasteries, and the highest motorable road on Earth at Khardung La.
Packages from ₹12,000/person
→

Yusmarg & Doodhpathri
Kashmir's best-kept secrets — pristine meadows of wildflowers, milky-white streams and pine forests untouched by mass tourism. Perfect for a quiet day escape from Srinagar.
Day trips from ₹800/person
